# Course Design Principles

## Core Philosophy
"AI is your tutor, not your ghostwriter. Use it to understand, not to skip understanding."

## Key Principles
1. **Simplification First:** Always simplify AI's complex solutions
2. **Understanding Over Completion:** Better to understand 5 lines than copy 50
3. **Reality Checks:** Regular demonstrations of AI limitations
4. **Trace Everything:** Students should trace execution by hand
5. **Resist Features:** Don't add features AI suggests until ready

## AI Integration Guidelines
- Use AI to explain concepts
- Use AI to debug WITH understanding
- Don't use AI to skip learning steps
- Always verify AI's code manually
